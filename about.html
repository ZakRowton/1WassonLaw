A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Kyle B. Wasson has dedicated his career to defending the rights of individuals facing criminal charges in Oklahoma. With over 18 years of legal experience, he brings a wealth of knowledge and a track record of success to every case.

Education & Background

Kyle earned his Bachelor of Arts degree from Texas State University in 1998. His commitment to justice and deep understanding of Oklahoma's legal system have made him a trusted advocate for clients throughout the state.

Professional Experience

Before founding Wasson Law, PLLC, Kyle practiced at Midtown Attorneys, where he gained extensive experience in criminal defense, DUI/DWI cases, wills and living wills, trusts, estate planning, and probate matters. This diverse background gives him a comprehensive perspective on the legal challenges his clients face.

Philosophy

At Wasson Law, we believe every person deserves a vigorous defense. Kyle approaches each case with the understanding that the outcome can profoundly impact his clients' lives, families, and futures. This commitment drives him to explore every legal avenue and fight for the best possible results.

"The law is not about winning at all costs - it's about ensuring justice is served and rights are protected." - Kyle B. Wasson
