DUI & DWI Defense

Being charged with driving under the influence can have serious consequences on your license, employment, and freedom. Kyle B. Wasson provides aggressive DUI defense, challenging:

  • Breathalyzer and blood test accuracy
  • Field sobriety test procedures
  • Traffic stop legality
  • License suspension hearings

Don't let a DUI charge derail your life. Contact us immediately to protect your driving privileges and fight the charges.
